## Known Limitations

1. Fixed 4-camera setup (no 5+ cameras)
2. No auto-calibration (manual YAML required)
3. No seam detection (uses full overlap)
4. No undistortion (assumes corrected cameras)
5. No pedestrian detection
6. No tone mapping
7. Fixed camera view (no mouse controls)
8. H.264 streams only (no other codecs)

## Technical Specifications

### Input
- **Cameras**: 4
- **Resolution**: 1280x800 (configurable)
- **Format**: H.264 over UDP
- **FOV**: 120° horizontal (configurable)
- **Frame Rate**: 30 FPS

### Processing
- **Warping**: Spherical projection
- **Blending**: Multi-band (5 levels)
- **Gain**: Channel-based compensation
- **Scale**: 0.65x (configurable)

### Output
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (configurable)
- **Format**: OpenGL texture
- **Display**: Real-time 3D bowl
- **Frame Rate**: 15-30 FPS (hardware dependent)
